# Venue Tiering

按领域分类会议/期刊优先级，供 `03-01-paper-bibliography` 等文献检索技能使用。

默认行为：先找 Tier A → 不足时扩展到 Tier B → 再扩展到 Tier C。只有用户明确要求 `top venues only` 时，才将 Tier A 作为硬过滤条件。

---

## ML / AI

### Tier A
**会议**：NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, CVPR, ICCV, ACL, EMNLP  
**期刊**：JMLR, TPAMI, IJCV

### Tier B
**会议**：AAAI, IJCAI, ECCV, NAACL, COLING, AISTATS, UAI  
**期刊**：Neural Networks, Pattern Recognition, MLJ

### Tier C
其他 AI/ML 相关会议 workshop、arXiv 高引 preprint

---

## 物理 / 复杂系统

### Tier A
**期刊**：Physical Review Letters (PRL), Physical Review X (PRX), Nature, Science  
**期刊**：Nature Physics, Nature Communications

### Tier B
**期刊**：Physical Review E (PRE), Physical Review B (PRB), Chaos, New Journal of Physics (NJP), Journal of Statistical Mechanics (JSTAT)

### Tier C
**期刊**：Journal of Physics A, Physical Letters A (PLA), Journal of Computational Physics (JCP), Chaos Solitons & Fractals

---

## 数值计算 / 计算科学

### Tier A
**期刊**：Journal of Computational Physics (JCP), Computer Physics Communications (CPC)

### Tier B
**期刊**：SIAM Journal on Scientific Computing (SISC), Computer Methods in Applied Mechanics and Engineering (CMAME), Journal of Scientific Computing

### Tier C
其他计算数学、数值方法相关期刊

---

## 通信 / 信号处理

### Tier A
**期刊**：IEEE JSAC, IEEE/ACM Transactions on Networking (ToN), IEEE TWC, IEEE TCOM  
**会议**：ACM SIGCOMM, USENIX NSDI, ACM MobiCom, IEEE INFOCOM

### Tier B
**期刊**：IEEE TVT, IEEE WCL, IEEE Communications Letters, Computer Networks  
**会议**：IEEE ICC, IEEE GLOBECOM, IEEE WCNC, ACM MobiHoc

### Tier C
其他 IEEE 通信/网络/信号处理相关期刊与会议
